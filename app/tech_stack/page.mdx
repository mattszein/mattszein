## Software 💾

After over a decade with Linux, I've settled into a setup that I enjoy and it works. I've tried various distributions over the years, mostly Debian based. Pop!_OS was my daily driver last years: it strikes the perfect balance between stability and staying out of my way.
But since 2025 Arch Linux is my choice and i love it. Now I'm using CachyOS (Arch based) using it with hyprland. It's lightweight, fast, and lets me customize everything to my liking.
<br />
For development, I live in neovim (lazyvim) and alacritty terminal. Neovim gives me the speed and customization I need. I use tmux for terminal multiplexing, which lets me manage multiple sessions easily. My shell is zsh with oh-my-zsh.
